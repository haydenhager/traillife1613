Adventure Awaits

Trail Life USA combines the traditional methods of the international scouting movement featuring outdoor experiences such as camping and hiking, the patrol system, development of leadership traits, community service, badge/award advancement and uniforms, all built upon the primary foundation of Christian character formation.

Christ-centered, Boy-focused

Trail Life USA is a Christ-centered, boy-focused outdoor adventure, character, and leadership program for young boys and men in elementary through high school.Boys will love the fun and adventure! Parents will love the commitment to a Christian worldview and moral consistency.
